Instructions to Authors

The authors willing to present papers in the conference are requested to submit the full-length paper not exceeding 6 pages (in IEEE format) by e-mail as an attachment to icseit2025@stellamaryscoe.edu.in. on or before the last date for submission. All the papers will be peer-reviewed by the experts. Authors of the selected paper will be intimated through e-mail.

The first author should incorporate the reviewers' comments and submit the camera-ready paper along with the registration form. The registration fee includes the conference kit, refreshments, and working lunch. Accommodation will be provided based on prior request with an additional cost.

Conference Proceedings

The ICSEIT 2K25 conference proceedings will include all the accepted papers and will be published in Scopus/WoS indexed journals. These papers will be peer-reviewed and selected based on their quality and relevance.

Publication Opportunities

Authors of accepted papers will have the opportunity to publish their papers in high-impact journals indexed by Scopus and Web of Science (WoS). The selected papers will undergo a final review process before publication.

How to Submit a Paper

Please refer to the "Call for Papers" section for detailed instructions on paper submission. Ensure your paper is in IEEE format and includes all necessary details. The registration fee also covers publication costs.

Publication FAQs

If you have any questions regarding the publication process or eligibility, please feel free to contact the conference organizers via email at icseit2025@stellamaryscoe.edu.in.
