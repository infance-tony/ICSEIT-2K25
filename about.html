About ICSEIT 2K25

About the Institution

Stella Mary's College of Engineering is promoted by the Nova Educational Trust & established by the well-known business entrepreneur of Kanyakumari District, Dr. Nazareth Charles (Founder and Chairman), an Engineer by profession and a great philanthropist working towards the upliftment of the poor, underprivileged, and the women of society. The institution creates skillful professionals to serve the nation and industry with integrity.

Stella Mary's offers a nurturing environment that facilitates excellence in academics, grooming leadership skills, and every feature a student needs for a successful career besides offering cutting-edge infrastructure. The global standards set at Stella Mary's in the field of teaching and research spur its relentless pursuit of excellence, nourishing and developing innovative and entrepreneurial skills. This enabled the institution to get accredited by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) and NBA (National Board of Accreditation) for its Mechanical and Computer Science Engineering departments at an early stage, within nine years of inception.

The institution has maintained an exceptional placement record, providing its students with ample opportunities to achieve career goals, including international placements.

About the Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Established in 2013, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) combines academic rigor with a commitment to technological advancement and innovation. The department provides students with hands-on experience through state-of-the-art laboratories, industry partnerships, and real-world projects, ensuring they are well-prepared for professional challenges in the tech industry.

The department is committed to nurturing students who excel in advanced mechanical technologies, fostering a culture of creativity and excellence. The Design and Fabrication Laboratory remains a cornerstone for groundbreaking projects that align with sustainable practices.

About the Department of Mechanical Engineering

Established in 2013, the Department of Mechanical Engineering combines academic rigor with a commitment to sustainability and innovation. The department provides students with hands-on experience through state-of-the-art laboratories, industry partnerships, and real-world projects, ensuring they are well-prepared for professional challenges.

The department is committed to nurturing students who excel in advanced mechanical technologies, fostering a culture of creativity and excellence. The Design and Fabrication Laboratory remains a cornerstone for groundbreaking projects that align with sustainable practices.

About the Department of AI & Data Science

The Department of AI & Data Science focuses on equipping students with expertise in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and big data analytics. Through industry-aligned curriculums and cutting-edge laboratories, the department prepares students for a future driven by AI advancements.

Students engage in innovative research and collaborative projects that address real-world problems, fostering their ability to think critically and develop impactful solutions for industries and society.

About the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering is committed to advancing technology and fostering innovation. It provides students with a solid foundation in electronics, communication systems, and embedded technologies, coupled with practical exposure to advanced tools and methodologies.

With a strong focus on research, the department encourages students to work on projects in areas such as IoT, wireless communications, and VLSI design, empowering them to contribute to technological advancements.

About the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications in power systems, renewable energy, and smart grids. Students are trained to design, develop, and optimize electrical systems to address modern challenges.

The department emphasizes sustainability and innovation, preparing students to lead in industries, research, and entrepreneurship while addressing energy demands responsibly.

About the Department of Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering is dedicated to building the infrastructure of the future. It provides students with a comprehensive education in structural engineering, environmental engineering, and construction management, along with hands-on experience in modern engineering practices.

With a strong focus on research, the department encourages students to work on projects in areas such as IoT, wireless communications, and VLSI design, empowering them to contribute to technological advancements.

Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in engineering education, research, and innovation, empowering professionals to address sustainable development challenges.

Mission

  • Deliver an exceptional learning environment that integrates theoretical and practical knowledge in mechanical engineering.
  • Encourage research and development aligned with sustainability and technological advancements.
  • Equip students to excel in global industries, academia, and entrepreneurial ventures.
